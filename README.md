# Who's That Pokemon? 🎮

Welcome to **Who's That Pokemon?**, a thrilling web-based quiz game inspired by the classic Pokemon TV show! 🐾 Test your Pokemon knowledge by guessing the names of Pokemon from their shadowy silhouettes. Each round features 5 random Pokemon, with a 10-second timer for each guess. Choose from multiple options and see how many you can get right!

## Features 🌟
- **Timed Challenges**: 10 seconds per question to keep the excitement high! ⏱️
- **Random Selection**: 5 different Pokemon chosen randomly each game.
- **Multiple Choice**: 4 options per question for fair gameplay.
- **Score Tracking**: See your final score out of 5 at the end.
- **Responsive Design**: Play on desktop or mobile devices.
- **Pokemon Silhouettes**: Classic black-and-white images for that nostalgic feel.

## How to Play 🎯
1. Click the "Start" button to begin the game.
2. Look at the silhouette and guess the Pokemon's name.
3. Select one of the four options before time runs out.
4. Click "Next" to proceed to the next question.
5. After 5 questions, view your score and restart if you want!

## Technologies Used 🛠️
- **HTML5**: Structure and layout.
- **CSS3**: Styling with gradients and animations.
- **JavaScript**: Game logic, timer, and interactivity.
- **Google Fonts**: Poppins font for a modern look.
